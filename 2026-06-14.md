# RCAT News — 2026-06-14

## 1. RCAT Stock Slides As $225M Equity Offering Hits Market
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** June 12, 2026
Red Cat Holdings dropped more than 13–14% after pricing a ~$225M common stock offering (a $200M underwritten deal plus a $30M over-allotment option) intended to fund acquisitions, R&D, capex, and working capital. Shares traded roughly toward the $9.40 offering level as traders repriced the dilution.

## 2. RCAT Stock Surges As Defense Deals And Price Targets Pile Up
**Source:** Timothy Sykes / timothysykes.com | **Date:** June 4, 2026

Analysts cited Red Cat's defense-focused drone and robotics portfolio and growing NATO and Asia-Pacific contract pipeline as key catalysts.

## 4. Red Cat Holdings Inc (RCAT) Expands Defense Portfolio With Drone and Maritime Growth
**Source:** Insider Monkey | **Date:** June 2026
Red Cat Holdings announced new international orders and two strategic partnerships, plus the acquisitions of Apium Swarm Robotics and the planned purchase of Quaze Technologies (pending Investment Canada Act clearance). New Black Widow™ drone orders were secured from a NATO ally through the NATO Support and Procurement Agency and from a second Asia-Pacific ally.

## 5. Red Cat Reports Q1 2026: Revenue Growth of 849% Y/Y, Gross Margin 12.7%
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** 2026
Red Cat Holdings posted Q1 2026 revenue of $15.5M, up 849% year-over-year, with gross profit of $2.0M and a gross margin of 12.7%. The company ended the quarter with $131.9M cash and $62.7M in inventory, reflecting its rapid scale-up to meet surging government demand.
