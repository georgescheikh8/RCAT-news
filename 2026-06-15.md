**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** 2026-06-01

## 4. RCAT Stock Jumps As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** 2026-05-27
Red Cat won a competitive contract to deliver 173 Black Widow small unmanned aircraft systems to the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force, including aircraft, ground control stations, training, and spare parts. The company simultaneously announced its planned acquisition of Quaze Technologies, sending shares surging from under $9 to close at $10.67.

## 5. RCAT Stock Pops As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** Timothy Sykes | **Date:** 2026-05-27
