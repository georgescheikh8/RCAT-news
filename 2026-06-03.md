# RCAT News — 2026-06-03

## 1. Roth Capital Initiates Red Cat With Buy Rating, $25 Target — Says Stock Can Rally Another 70%
**Source:** CNBC / Investing.com | **Date:** June 1, 2026
Roth Capital analyst Craig Irwin initiated coverage on Red Cat Holdings (RCAT) with a Buy rating and a $25 price target, calling the company "well positioned for breakout growth from an expanding drone portfolio and intense demand." Irwin noted the production footprint is designed for $1 billion in revenue vs. the 2026 guide of $150–$180 million, with DoD and allied procurements providing upside visibility.

## 2. RCAT Stock Surges 9% on Japan Deal, Quaze Acquisition, and Bullish Analyst Coverage
**Source:** StocksToTrade / ECIKS.org | **Date:** May 27, 2026
Red Cat Holdings jumped 9.21% to $10.67 after Japan's Acquisition, Technology & Logistics Agency awarded a contract for 173 Black Widow small unmanned aircraft systems to the Japanese Ground Self-Defense Force for tactical ISR missions. The move came alongside the newly closed Quaze Technologies acquisition and fresh bullish analyst initiations, fueling strong investor momentum.

## 3. Red Cat Closes Acquisition of Quaze Technologies
**Source:** GlobeNewswire / Red Cat IR | **Date:** May 20, 2026
Red Cat Holdings completed its all-stock acquisition of Quaze Technologies, a Québec-based developer of wireless power transfer technology for unmanned systems, valuing the deal at approximately $21 million with up to $5 million in performance-based earnout shares. Quaze will operate as an independent Red Cat business unit and continue developing its wireless charging architecture for integration across Red Cat's Family of Systems.

## 4. Red Cat Holdings Completes $225M Public Stock Offering
**Source:** Globe and Mail / TipRanks | **Date:** May 14, 2026
Red Cat Holdings closed an underwritten public offering of 23,936,171 shares at $9.40 per share, raising approximately $225 million in gross proceeds, with Evercore Group and BofA Securities acting as underwriters. The company stated proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes including acquisitions, R&D, and working capital to accelerate strategic growth initiatives.

## 5. Red Cat Reports Q1 2026: Revenue Growth of 849% Y/Y, Gross Margins Surge
**Source:** GlobeNewswire / Red Cat IR | **Date:** May 7, 2026
Red Cat Holdings reported Q1 2026 revenue of $15.5 million, up 849% year-over-year from $1.6 million, with gross margin improving dramatically to 12.7% from -52.1% in the prior-year quarter. The company also announced new Black Widow drone orders from a NATO ally via the NATO Support and Procurement Agency and from a second Asia-Pacific ally, signaling strong international demand.
