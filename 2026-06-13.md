## 1. RCAT Stock Slides As $225M Equity Offering Hits Market

## 4. Red Cat Holdings (RCAT) Partners on U.S. Army Threat Analysis Kit
**Source:** GuruFocus | **Date:** June 2, 2026

## 5. Red Cat Q1 Revenue Jumps 849% to $15.5M
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** May 2026
