# RCAT News — 2026-06-06

## 1. RCAT Stock Surges As Defense Deals And Price Targets Pile Up
**Source:** Timothy Sykes | **Date:** 2026-06-04
RCAT surged 7.59% on June 4 as H.C. Wainwright initiated coverage with a Buy rating and $20 price target, followed by Roth Capital issuing a Buy/$25 target citing "breakout growth" potential. The Trump administration's proposed $1.1B "Drone Dominance" program — aimed at producing 300,000 low-cost attack drones by 2027 — added further bullish momentum.

## 2. RCAT Stock Jumps As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** 2026-05-27
RCAT closed at $10.67 then spiked to $14.15 after the company announced it would supply 173 Black Widow small unmanned aircraft systems to Japan's Ground Self-Defense Force for FY2026. The Quaze acquisition also bolstered investor confidence, lifting Red Cat alongside broader strength in U.S. defense drone stocks.

## 3. Red Cat Q1 Revenue Jumps 849% to $15.5M, Margin 12.7%
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** 2026-05-15
Red Cat Holdings reported Q1 2026 revenue of $15.5 million, up 849% year-over-year, driven by surging defense contract demand for its Black Widow drone systems. Gross profit came in at $2.0 million with a 12.7% gross margin as the company scales production.

## 4. Red Cat Holdings Completes $225M Public Stock Offering
**Source:** TipRanks | **Date:** 2026-05-14
Red Cat Holdings closed a public offering of approximately 23.9 million shares at $9.40 per share, raising gross proceeds of roughly $225 million to fund continued expansion. The capital raise is expected to support manufacturing scale-up and pursuit of additional government contracts.

## 5. RCAT Stock Pressured As Red Cat Unloads $225M Equity Deal
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** 2026-05-13
RCAT came under selling pressure after announcing a ~23.9 million share public offering at $9.40, raising dilution concerns among existing shareholders. The $225M raise is earmarked for growth in the defense drone market, but near-term dilution weighed on the stock at announcement.
