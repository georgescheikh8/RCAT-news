## 1. RCAT Stock Slides As $225M Equity Offering Hits Market

**Source:** CoinCentral | **Date:** June 2026
Red Cat unveiled its new Hellcat drone at Eurosatory 2026 in Paris, expanding its defense-focused small UAS portfolio. Shares rebounded pre-market following the product reveal, signaling continued investor interest in the company's growing drone lineup.

## 3. RCAT Stock Surges As Defense Deals And Price Targets Pile Up
**Source:** Timothy Sykes | **Date:** June 4, 2026

## 4. Red Cat Q1 Revenue Jumps 849% to $15.5M, Margin 12.7%
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** June 2026
Red Cat Holdings reported Q1 2026 revenue of $15.5 million, up 849% year-over-year, and gross profit of $2.0 million with a gross margin of 12.7% versus (52.1)% a year earlier. The results reflect rapid commercialization of its Black Widow and Teal 2 drone systems.

## 5. RCAT Stock Jumps As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** May 27, 2026
Red Cat secured a contract to supply 173 Black Widow small UAS to the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force following a competitive ATLA-led process. The company also acquired Quebec-based Quaze Technologies for wireless power transfer, integrating autonomous charging across its drone and robotics platforms.
