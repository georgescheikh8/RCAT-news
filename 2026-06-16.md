# RCAT News — 2026-06-16

## 1. RCAT Stock Slides As $225M Equity Offering Hits Market
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** June 12, 2026
Red Cat Holdings dropped more than 13–14% after the company announced a $225M equity offering, with traders repricing the stock toward the $9.40 offering level. The dilution spooked momentum buyers who had driven the stock up sharply in prior weeks.

**Source:** Timothy Sykes | **Date:** June 4, 2026

**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** June 2026

## 4. RCAT Stock Jumps As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** May 27, 2026

## 5. RCAT Stock Pops As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** Timothy Sykes | **Date:** May 27, 2026
Red Cat Holdings stock spiked from under $9 to a high of $14.15 by May 28, a move of more than 70% in just a few weeks, driven by the Japan defense contract and Quaze acquisition news. The stock later pulled back to the low $11s as the initial euphoria faded.
