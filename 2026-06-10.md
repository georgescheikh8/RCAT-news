## 2. Red Cat Q1 Revenue Jumps 849% to $15.5M, Gross Margin Turns Positive
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** 2026-05-28
Red Cat Holdings reported Q1 2026 revenue of $15.5 million, up 849% year-over-year, with gross margin improving dramatically to 12.7% from -52.1% a year ago. The blowout results underscore the company's rapid transition from development-stage to revenue-generating defense contractor.

## 3. RCAT Stock Pops As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** Timothy Sykes / StocksToTrade | **Date:** 2026-05-27
Red Cat announced it will supply 173 Black Widow small unmanned aircraft systems to the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force for FY2026 following a competitive ATLA-led procurement process. The company also acquired Québec-based Quaze Technologies for wireless power transfer to enable autonomous drone charging across its platforms.

## 5. Red Cat Priming For Drone Leadership
**Source:** Seeking Alpha | **Date:** 2026-05-10
A deep-dive analysis on Seeking Alpha argues that Red Cat Holdings is well-positioned to become a leading U.S. defense drone supplier, buoyed by the Trump administration's proposed $1.1 billion "Drone Dominance" program targeting 300,000 low-cost attack drones by 2027.
