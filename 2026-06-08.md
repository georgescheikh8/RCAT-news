## 2. Red Cat Q1 Revenue Jumps 849% to $15.5M, Gross Margin 12.7%
**Source:** StockTitan | **Date:** June 2026

## 3. RCAT Stock Jumps As Japan Deal And Quaze Acquisition Fuel Momentum
**Source:** StocksToTrade | **Date:** May 27, 2026
Red Cat Holdings won a contract to supply 173 Black Widow small unmanned aircraft systems to the Japan Ground Self-Defense Force for FY2026 following a competitive ATLA-led selection process. The company also acquired Quebec-based Quaze Technologies, which specializes in wireless power transfer for drones.
